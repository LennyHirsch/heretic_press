My designs are hand-printed by myself on Awagami Bunkoshi Select paper. This paper is a fine Japanese paper with a weight of 70gsm made of 30% Kozo and 70% Alpha Cellulose. It is an extremely high-quality paper produced especially for printmaking with a smooth finish and a subtle off-white colour.

Other papers may be available by request.
